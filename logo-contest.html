JSON for Linked Data

JSON-LD Logo Contest

JSON-LD started as a community project and will (hopefully soon) become an official W3C standard. The time has come for a re-branding and you can contribute by proposing the new JSON-LD logo (plus points for a redesign of our homepage).

Your proposal link will be published on the JSON-LD wiki next to the other proposals. On a date to be specified the community will choose the new logo through voting on the JSON-LD mailing list.

Guidelines

The logo should meet the requirements below:

  • Two versions of the logo: a rectangle (full brand) and a square (icon, for example)
  • Prepared to be scaled up or down
  • Translate to a 2 color look (black/white, 2 greys, etc)
  • Ability to be inverted if running on a dark background

Note: The design as accepted by popular vote will be treated as a proposal that can be tweaked and cemented into it's final published form.

Inspiration

While designing the logo, keep in mind what describes JSON-LD:

  • Simplicity
  • Expressitivity
  • Ubiquity / cross-platform
  • Linked Data (graphs of data)
  • Web APIs
  • Community Driven

More information about what JSON-LD is, can be found on the homepage.

Deadline

March 24th, 2013

How to contribute

Send to the JSON-LD mailing list (public-linked-json@w3.org) a message with [LOGO] in the subject (like "*[LOGO] Joe Designer*") and the URLs of:

  • A PNG version of the logo (and its scalable variations)
  • A ZIP file with all assets

Prize

The winning contributor will receive recognition for their logo on the official JSON-LD website. The contributor will also have taken part in defining JSON-LD as a community first project.

License

The authors of the selected JSON-LD logo(s) will need to donate them to the JSON-LD project according to the W3C Community Contributor License Agreement (CLA).
